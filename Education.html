Primary and Secondary Education

I attended 5 different primary schools from the age of 6, a mixture of rural countryside and ghetto areas. It was during secondary school where I developed a more passionate approach for football. Namely in secondary school, I met Mr. Reginald Chirapa, the man who was my woodwork teacher in school, coach at the soccer ground, and a friend and mentor outside the school yard. I completed part of my A Level studies (high school) at Milton High School in Bulawayo after being signed by the now defunct AmaZulu Fc, which were playing in Zimbabwean premier league.

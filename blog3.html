DISCIPLINE IS A VITAL PIECE WHEN IT COMES TO THE GAME.

Be aware of the requirements of your sport. 90 minutes on the field of play is a continuation of the process. The whole package is made up of your daily activities from the time you wake up till you go to bed. The targets you set for yourself provide you with a clearer picture of what you want to achieve and encourage self-motivation. The coaches are there to guide you and prepare the tactics. But the willingness and caring of your body have to come from yourself because you are the one who carries your own body and understands your feelings better.

DISCIPLINE IS A VITAL PIECE WHEN IT COMES TO THE GAME.

To make life easier at a professional soccer level, maintain your focus. A lot of distractions and temptations are present. Always remember where you are coming from so that you don’t lose track of where you are going.

“If you know your history, then you would know where you are coming from”~ Bob Marley

The lifestyle you had before turning to a professional soccer player should serve as a reminder of what’s valuable for you in life. Maintain your focus on goals of becoming better just like before you turned professional. If you make it at a higher level, then you have to remember that you have automatically become an ambassador of your origin, a ghetto representative, and you would want to show the other youths a better path.

Think about those that you left behind, those that couldn’t make it because of extenuating circumstances, those that had and have aspiring ambitions like you, those newly upcoming stars. The way you handle yourself, and maintain the focus could be part of their revival or strength, be it physically, emotionally or socially. I made it to higher professional soccer levels not because I am the best, I believe that there are many out there with exceptional skills but could not get the chance. The best I can do is utilize my opportunity well by keeping my focus on the game and on my behaviour. That way kids back in the ghetto will have something to look up to and cherish. Maintain your focus to stay on the right path.
